# Task A Prompt Template

You are given a fictional Chinese dialogue scene.
Your job is to analyze the speaker's likely emotional state, subtext, motive, and relationship logic.

Return concise but specific answers for:
1. literal meaning
2. primary emotion
3. secondary emotions
4. speaker goal
5. speaker fear / defense
6. subtext
7. relationship logic
8. what a good in-character reply should preserve
9. what a common but wrong reply would get wrong

Do not default to generic emotional-support language. Respect the fictional relationship and setting.
